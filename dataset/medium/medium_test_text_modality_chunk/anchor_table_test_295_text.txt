ENTERPRISE PRODCT PARTNRS LP report:
Gross operating margin from our NGL pipelines, storage and terminal assets during 2019 increased $353.9 million when compared to 2018. Shin Oak contributed $112.0 million of gross operating margin in 2019 on year-to-date transportation volumes of (i) 107 MBPD of direct tariff movements (net to our interest) and (ii) 129 MBPD of offload volumes from an affiliate pipeline. Gross operating margin from our Mont Belvieu storage facility increased $106.4 million year-to-year primarily due to higher throughput and handling fees, which accounted for an $81.0 million increase, and higher storage fees, which accounted for an additional $24.3 million increase.
Gross operating margin from LPG-related activities at EHT increased $41.0 million year-to-year primarily due to higher net export volumes of 45 MBPD, which accounted for a $33.2 million increase, and lower operating costs, which accounted for an additional $7.8 million increase. Gross operating margin from our Houston Ship Channel Pipeline System increased $ 10.6 million year-to-year primarily due to higher transportation volumes, which accounted for $7.0 million of the increase, and higher average transportation fees, which accounted for an additional $5.0 million increase. Transportation volumes on our Houston Ship Channel Pipeline System increased 44 MBPD primarily due to the LPG expansion at EHT that was completed during the third quarter of 2019. 
Gross operating margin from our Aegis Pipeline increased $40.9 million year-to-year primarily due to higher transportation volumes of 72 MBPD. Gross operating margin from our Dixie Pipeline and related terminals increased a combined $24.7 million year-to-year primarily due to higher transportation volumes of 23 MBPD, which accounted for an $11.5 million increase, and lower operating costs, which accounted for an additional $10.5 million increase.
